LOST FOREST

Explore the mysterious Lost Forest, a lush area filled with diverse flora and fauna. This is a perfect spot for nature walks and bird watching.

Recommended activities:

  • 7:30 am – Guided nature walk
  • 10:00 am – Bird watching session
  • 2:00 pm – Educational talk on forest conservation

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest is located north of the main visitor center, past the Butterfly Pavilion.
