ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Experience the lively paths of the forest as you follow the Monkey Trail. Enjoy watching playful monkeys in their natural habitat and learn about their behavior.

Trail highlights:

  • 8:00 am : Guided tour starts
  • 10:30 am : Feeding time observation
  • 1:00 pm : Educational talk about primates

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is located just east of the visitor center, near the riverbank.
