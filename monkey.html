MONKEYS

Monkeys are highly intelligent primates found in various regions around the world, including Africa, Asia, Central and South America. There are two major types of monkeys: Old World monkeys, which live in Africa and Asia, and New World monkeys, found in the Americas.

Monkeys are known for their agility, expressive faces, and advanced problem-solving abilities. They are social animals that live in groups and communicate through vocalizations, facial expressions, and body movements. Their diets vary widely, including fruits, leaves, insects, and even small animals.

Some species have prehensile tails that help them grasp branches, especially among New World monkeys like spider monkeys. Monkeys are active and playful, making them one of the most engaging species to observe at the zoo.
